About Us

Background:
IIT Bombay has always taken pride in welcoming and enhancing forthcoming research. The industry today has significant problems at hand along with ample capital to disburse and institutions such as ours hold the brightest minds and state of the art R&D capabilities to bring out ground-breaking innovations in the sector. The problem here is: Information Gap and the solution: Metals and Materials Summit

Why Metals and Materials Summit?
It is an initiative focused on closing in the gap between academia and Industry, by addressing the information and bringing in all stakeholders under the same roof to start discussions on transformative technologies. It will involve a blend of Metallurgical engineering based competitions, lectures, exhibitions, workshops and many fun-with-learning events.
